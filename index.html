Welcome to SIRTE

Sagar Institute of Research and Technology Excellence (SIRTE), Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh was established in the year 2005 and is one of the top engineering colleges of Madhya Pradesh. The institute is a part of Sagar Group of Institutions and is a sister college of Sagar Institute of Research and Technology. The institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and is affiliated with RGPV University, Bhopal. The Institute offers Undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and MBA programs to eligible students. The basic admission criteria to the Institute is based on different entrance tests for different courses.
